My Goals

june 8 2017

I began programming as young as 9 or 10 just creating some simple batch files to do some very simple tasks. I eventually took over C++ years later and created some complex applications to push my creativity and logic thinking skills to the limit; however, after some time i decided to move on and learn javascript, and with javascript came HTML, CSS, and JQuery. Although I didn't just decide to learn to program as only just a hobby though, i had some goals in mind. I wanted to be able to program quickly, professionally, and write some impressive code along the way. These goals never left me, and i still aspire to become a very strong programmer, and never stop learning. There is a never ending amount of information out in the world, and i love discovering every little bit of it. Programming has been in my life since the beginning , and it will stay here for the rest of my life while i head toward my never ending goal to become a better programmer.

My ideology to keep learning isn't unnusual though, i beleive every programmer out there should have this mentality, without it, how else are we meant to imporve? Everyone should set a goal to continue to improve, and become the very best that they can. There are plenty of resources available to help imporve with logical thinking, learning helpful algorithims or learning amazing new frameworks, Plus teaching yourself things like machine learning, and descrete math can drastically change the way you think about computer science and programming. I recommend all programmers to spend some time every day to learn just a little bit more.
